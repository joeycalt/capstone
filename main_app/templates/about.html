About myBudget

Welcome to myBudget!

Thank you for visiting our site myBudget! We are very fortunate to have such a smart person like you to be here! Just in case you don't know what this site is about, we will explain to you right here. Here at myBudget, we help people like you budget themselves to help save money and track their purchases. Every purchase you make, at myBudget you will be able to input all the information of it and compare it to the budget you set yourself for the month. You will be able to sign in and revisit the budget information to see where you're at and if that next purchase is the right thing to do. Best part is, its all FREE! Thank you again for visiting our page, if you have any questions or concerns just visit our contact page.
